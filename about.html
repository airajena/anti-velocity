About PetConnect

Driven by our motto, No Animal Left Behind, we rescue, care for, and connect animals with loving homes.

Join Our Mission Support Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate animal lovers, PetConnect began with a simple goal: to save stray, abandoned, and injured animals. Our No Animal Left Behind philosophy has guided us to rescue over 2,000 animals and facilitate hundreds of adoptions.

We work closely with communities, veterinarians, and volunteers to provide medical care, shelter, and love to every animal in need. Our journey is fueled by compassion and a commitment to creating a world where every pet has a home.

Get Involved

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome every stray, abandoned, or injured animal, ensuring they receive the care and love they deserve.

Our Vision

A world where no animal is left behind, and every pet thrives in a safe, loving home with a supportive community.

Our Journey

  • 2018: PetConnect founded, rescued 70 animals in our first year.
  • 2019: Celebrated 100 adoptions and established local vet partnerships.
  • 2020: Launched community outreach programs to support street feeders.
  • 2022: Opened our own care and training center for rescued animals.
  • 2024: Surpassed 2,000 animals rescued and expanded volunteer network.
  • 2025: Introduced mobile vet clinics to reach underserved areas.
