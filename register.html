Why Register a Stray?

Save Lives

Reporting strays helps us provide medical care, shelter, and adoption opportunities.

Ensure Safety

Urgent cases, like injured animals, are prioritized for immediate rescue.

Build Community

Your report contributes to a network of compassionate animal lovers.

What Happens Next?

Step 1: We Review

Our team assesses your submission, prioritizing urgent cases based on medical needs or danger.

Step 2: Rescue & Care

We dispatch a rescue team to the location, provide medical treatment, and place the animal in a safe environment.

Step 3: Updates

You’ll receive email updates on the animal’s status, including health improvements and adoption progress.

Step 4: Forever Home

The animal is prepared for adoption or fostering, finding a loving home through our network.
